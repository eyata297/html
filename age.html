cheesy pizza

A cheese pizza is a pizza with only pizza dough, tomato sauce, and cheese as toppings.

It is a basic type of pizza, often featuring mozzarella, and can sometimes include other cheeses like parmesan.

A cheese pizza is also known as a plain pizza and can be a simplified version of more complex pizzas like a Margherita or Quattro Formaggi (four cheese pizza).

It is cheesy and delicious.

peperoni pizza

Pepperoni pizza is an American-Italian dish consisting of a pizza crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and spicy, cured slices of pepperoni.

Pepperoni itself is a salami-like meat made from a mixture of pork and beef, seasoned with paprika or other peppers, giving it a tangy and spicy flavor that is a staple topping in American pizzerias.
